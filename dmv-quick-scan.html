CO DMV - Find Soonest Appointment

You're on iPhone so the easiest method is the bookmarklet. Follow the steps below.
Step 1 - Open the DMV appointment page

Step 2 - On that page, select your service type first (Reinstatement / Financial Responsibility)
Step 3 - Then manually check each location by selecting it from the dropdown. The calendar will show the first available date highlighted.

Check these offices (closest to Westminster):

1. Westminster (0 min drive)
2. Lakewood (20 min) ← usually shortest wait!
3. Golden (25 min)
4. Denver Central (25 min)
5. Denver - Peoria (30 min)
6. Boulder (35 min)
7. Aurora (35 min)
